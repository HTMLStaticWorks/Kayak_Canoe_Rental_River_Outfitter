Be Prepared

Safety & Trip Preparation

We want every adventure to end with smiles. Follow our pre-trip checklist for a safe, fun experience.

Check River Levels

Always check current CFS flow rates before launching. We text conditions to all confirmed bookers.

Dress for the Water

Wear clothes that dry fast. Wetsuit rentals available May–June. Sandals with straps recommended.

Pack Smart

Waterproof dry bags, sunscreen, snacks for long trips, ID and emergency contact card in a ziploc.

Save Our Number

Save (555) 748-3001 in your phone before you launch. Our guide dispatch is monitored 24/7.
