FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book?
We recommend booking at least 2-3 days in advance for weekdays and 7+ days for weekends (Memorial Day to Labor Day). Group bookings (10+) should book 3-4 weeks in advance to guarantee equipment and guide availability.
Do you run trips in rainy weather?
Yes, we run trips in drizzle and light rain — it's often the best time to be on the river! We cancel only for lightning, severe thunderstorms or flash flood warnings. If we cancel due to weather, you receive a full refund or priority rescheduling.
Can you accommodate guests with disabilities?
Absolutely. We have adaptive gear and accessible launches for many conditions. Please contact us directly before booking to discuss your specific needs — we'll do everything we can to make your river experience safe and joyful.
How do I get a group quote?
Fill in the contact form above or email hello@riverrunoutfitters.com with your group size, preferred dates and the type of experience you're looking for. We'll get back to you within 24 hours with a custom quote.
