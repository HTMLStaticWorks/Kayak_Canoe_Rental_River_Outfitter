Experience

Why This Trip?

The Emerald Valley Float isn't just a trip; it's an immersion into the wild heart of the Pacific Northwest.

Ancient Forests

Navigate through 500-year-old Douglas fir stands that tower over the crystal clear river banks.

Crystal Waters

The McKenzie is world-famous for its clarity. See every stone on the riverbed from your boat.

Wildlife Spotting

Keep your eyes peeled for Ospreys, Bald Eagles, and the occasional black bear at the water's edge.

Timeline

Your 3-Hour Adventure

1
The Launch (Mile 0)

After a brief safety overview and gear check, we launch from Rattlesnake Bridge. The first mile is calm, allowing you to find your rhythm.

2
The Canyon Bend (Mile 3)

The river narrows into a scenic canyon. Expect gentle riffles and stunning basalt wall formations. A perfect spot for photos!

3
Cedar Falls Take-out (Mile 7)

We drift into the take-out at the base of Cedar Falls. Our shuttle team will be waiting to bring you back to the base camp.
