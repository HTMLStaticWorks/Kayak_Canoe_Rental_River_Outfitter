Trip Overview

What to Expect

The Emerald Valley Float is our most popular guided trip, and for good reason. You'll launch from the Rattlesnake Bridge put-in and drift through 7 miles of stunning old-growth Douglas fir and western red cedar forest, ending at Cedar Falls take-out.

The river runs Class I-II throughout, making it perfect for first-time paddlers, families with kids 6+, and anyone who wants to enjoy Oregon's legendary river scenery at a relaxed pace.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior kayaking experience?
No experience is needed! Our guide provides a complete paddling lesson at the put-in. The Emerald Valley Float is Class I-II â€” flat water, gentle riffles, and very friendly for beginners and families.
What is the minimum age requirement?
Children 6 and older are welcome on the Emerald Valley Float when accompanied by a parent or guardian. Children 4-5 may participate in calm-water canoe rentals with a parent. For whitewater trips, minimum age is 14+.
What happens if we get rained on?
Light rain is part of the adventure! We run trips in all but severe weather (lightning, flash flood warnings). We monitor conditions hourly and will contact you 24 hours in advance if we need to reschedule. You will receive a full refund or credit if we cancel due to weather.
Can I bring my dog?
Yes! Well-behaved dogs are welcome on the Emerald Valley Float and most self-guided rentals. Dogs must have a canine PFD (we stock size S-XL for rent at $8/day). Please declare your pet when booking.
Is there a cancellation policy?
Full refund for cancellations made 48+ hours before the trip. 50% refund for cancellations 24-48 hours before. No refund for cancellations less than 24 hours before the trip. Travel insurance is strongly recommended for large group bookings.
