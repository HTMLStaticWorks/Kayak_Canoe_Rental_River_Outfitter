Our Story

Born on the River,
Built for Adventurers

AquaGo was born in the summer of 2009 when founders Marcus Webb and Elena Sorensen paddled the entire length of the McKenzie River in a borrowed canoe. What started as a personal adventure quickly became a calling.

Seeing how many people wanted to experience the river but lacked the equipment, knowledge or confidence to do so safely, Marcus and Elena founded RiverRun with a simple mission: make river adventures accessible, safe and unforgettable for everyone.

Our Mission

Connecting People
with Wild Rivers

We believe that everyone — from curious first-timers to seasoned experts — deserves the chance to experience the wonder of wild rivers. Our mission drives every decision we make.

Accessibility: Making river adventures available to all ages, backgrounds and skill levels.
Safety: Every guide, every piece of gear and every route designed with safety as priority #1.
Stewardship: Protecting the rivers we love through Leave No Trace practices and active restoration.

Green Pledge

Our Environmental Commitment

The river sustains us. We're dedicated to protecting the ecosystems that make these adventures possible for future generations.

1 Booking = 1 Tree

We partner with Oregon ReForest Project. Every paid booking plants a native tree in riparian zones along river corridors.

Zero Waste Operations

All gear is repaired and recycled, never landfilled. Our outpost runs on 100% solar power and uses biodegradable soaps only.

River Cleanup Days

Monthly volunteer river cleanups — customers paddle free in exchange for 2 hours of river cleanup. Over 4 tons removed since 2012.

Our Journey

15 Years on the River

09
2009 — The Beginning

Marcus and Elena launch RiverRun with 4 kayaks, 2 canoes and a borrowed truck. First season: 400 rentals.

12
2012 — First Guided Trips

Launched guided tour program with first ACA-certified guide hire. River cleanup program begins.

15
2015 — Shuttle Service Launch

Added point-to-point shuttle, opening 8 new river routes and growing fleet to 60 boats.

19
2019 — 10 Year Milestone

Celebrated 10,000th customer. Won Oregon Tourism Excellence Award. Hired full-time naturalist.

26
2026 — Today & Growing

100+ vessels, 12+ guides, 48 routes across 3 rivers. 12,500+ adventurers served and counting!
